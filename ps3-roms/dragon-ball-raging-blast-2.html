Dragon Ball - Raging Blast 2 Review

Released in 2010 by Bandai Namco Entertainment, Dragon Ball: Raging Blast 2 is the direct sequel to Dragon Ball: Raging Blast, and continues to build upon the high-flying, action-packed battles that fans of the iconic anime series have come to love. Available on the PlayStation 3, the game brings together a roster of classic characters, fast-paced combat, and stunning visuals to deliver a gameplay experience that immerses players in the Dragon Ball universe. In this review, we will dive deep into the game's mechanics, story, graphics, and overall experience to assess how it stands as a worthy sequel in the franchise's long history of video games.

Introduction

As a follow-up to its predecessor, Raging Blast 2 improves on the formula established in the first game while introducing new features and refining the combat mechanics. Players can once again take control of some of the most iconic characters in the Dragon Ball universe, from Goku and Vegeta to lesser-known fighters, all while engaging in epic, fast-paced battles that span multiple locations from the anime. The game is designed to appeal to both casual fans and diehard Dragon Ball enthusiasts, offering a mix of accessible gameplay and more advanced mechanics for competitive play.

Gameplay

Dragon Ball: Raging Blast 2 retains much of the gameplay style from its predecessor, with its primary focus on 3D arena combat. Players can freely move around the battlefield, engage in combo-based attacks, and unleash devastating special moves. While the game's core mechanics remain similar, there are several significant improvements and new features that enhance the overall experience.

Combat System

The combat in Raging Blast 2 is fast, dynamic, and over-the-top, staying true to the source material. The gameplay revolves around executing powerful combos, using ki blasts, and activating signature moves like the Kamehameha or Final Flash. The game’s controls are intuitive enough for newcomers to pick up, but they also offer depth for experienced players looking to master advanced techniques.

  • Attack Combos: Players can string together light and heavy attacks into powerful combos that can overwhelm opponents. The game’s combat system allows players to execute quick and fluid combinations, making battles fast-paced and exciting.
  • Ki Blasts: Ki blasts are a key part of the game, and players can charge up and unleash devastating long-range attacks. The ki system requires players to balance offensive and defensive tactics, as it’s necessary to manage energy during the fight.
  • Special Moves: Each character in the game has a set of unique special moves, such as Goku’s Kamehameha and Vegeta’s Final Flash. These attacks are visually spectacular and can turn the tide of battle if used correctly.
  • Ultimate Attacks: Ultimate attacks are the signature finishing moves in the game, triggered when certain conditions are met. These attacks often deal massive damage and are one of the most exciting aspects of the combat system.

Battle Arenas

The battle arenas in Raging Blast 2 are large and diverse, providing plenty of space for players to engage in fast, fluid battles. The stages are inspired by locations from the Dragon Ball universe, such as the World Tournament Arena, Namek, and the Hyperbolic Time Chamber. Each arena has its own distinct features, and the environments play a role in how players approach combat. For example, certain stages have destructible environments, which can add an extra layer of strategy, as players can use the surroundings to their advantage.

Game Modes

The game offers a wide variety of modes, catering to both single-player and multiplayer experiences. Some of the main game modes include:

  • Story Mode: The story mode follows the events of the Dragon Ball Z series, with players able to control various characters from the series and engage in battles inspired by key story arcs. This mode allows players to relive some of the most iconic moments from the anime while also introducing new scenarios.
  • Mission Mode: Mission mode gives players the opportunity to complete a series of challenges and objectives, such as defeating specific enemies or performing specific moves. This mode offers more varied content than the traditional story mode and encourages players to master the game's combat mechanics.
  • Online Mode: The online multiplayer mode lets players compete against others across the globe in ranked and unranked matches. Players can select their favorite characters and engage in one-on-one battles, testing their skills against others.
  • Local Multiplayer: For those who prefer to play with friends, the game also includes local multiplayer modes, allowing two players to fight against each other using split-screen or single-screen gameplay.

Character Roster

Dragon Ball: Raging Blast 2 features an extensive roster of characters, including fan favorites such as Goku, Vegeta, and Frieza, as well as lesser-known characters from the Dragon Ball universe. The game includes over 90 playable characters, ensuring that there is a fighter for every type of player.

New Additions and Customization

The roster in Raging Blast 2 expands upon the previous game with the inclusion of new characters, such as Kid Goku and Android 18. Additionally, each character has their own set of customizable moves, allowing players to personalize their fighter’s abilities. This adds a layer of depth to the game, as players can tailor their favorite characters to suit their preferred playstyle.

Graphics and Presentation

One of the standout features of Dragon Ball: Raging Blast 2 is its stunning visuals. The game captures the look and feel of the Dragon Ball anime series with impressive attention to detail. The character models are faithfully recreated, and the vibrant, colorful environments are a joy to explore. The animations for attacks, special moves, and Ultimate attacks are fluid and visually spectacular, making each battle feel like an action-packed episode of the show.

Character Models and Effects

The character models in Raging Blast 2 are highly detailed and closely resemble their anime counterparts. The in-game animations for attacks, ki blasts, and transformations are fluid, with characters moving and reacting in ways that feel true to the show. The visual effects for special moves and Ultimate attacks are particularly impressive, with explosive impacts and bright energy effects adding a sense of excitement to every battle.

Audio Design

The audio in Raging Blast 2 complements the game’s visual style. The soundtrack features a mixture of fast-paced tracks that accompany the intense combat, as well as more atmospheric pieces that play during the game’s menus and story sections. Voice acting from the original Japanese cast adds authenticity to the experience, and the sound effects, such as the crackling of energy blasts and the impact of punches, further immerse players in the world of Dragon Ball.

Replayability and Longevity

Dragon Ball: Raging Blast 2 offers a considerable amount of content, ensuring that players have plenty of reasons to keep coming back. The large character roster, numerous game modes, and variety of challenges in Mission Mode provide hours of gameplay. The inclusion of online multiplayer further extends the game’s replay value, allowing players to test their skills against others worldwide.

Unlockables and Achievements

In addition to the main game modes, there are a variety of unlockables in Raging Blast 2. Players can unlock new characters, stages, and alternate costumes as they progress through the game, adding to the sense of progression. Completing achievements and trophies further incentivizes players to explore the game’s content and master its mechanics.

Multiplayer Replayability

The online multiplayer mode is one of the game’s key features, offering players the chance to compete against others for rankings and rewards. The game’s competitive nature ensures that players will continue to face off against different opponents, improving their skills and discovering new strategies. The local multiplayer mode also provides hours of entertainment, especially for fans who enjoy playing with friends or family.

Conclusion

Dragon Ball: Raging Blast 2 is a worthy addition to the Dragon Ball video game franchise. The game successfully captures the essence of the anime series, with exciting combat, stunning visuals, and a vast roster of characters. The improvements made over the original game, including enhanced graphics, more varied stages, and a deeper combat system, make this an enjoyable experience for both newcomers and veteran players alike.

While the game is not without its flaws—such as occasional repetitive combat and limited story mode depth—Dragon Ball: Raging Blast 2 still delivers an engaging and entertaining experience that will satisfy fans of the series. Whether playing alone or with friends, the game offers plenty of content and replay value, making it a must-play for any Dragon Ball enthusiast.
