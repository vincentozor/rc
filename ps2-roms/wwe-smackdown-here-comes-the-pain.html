About WWE SmackDown! Here Comes the Pain (PS2)

Released in 2003 for the PlayStation 2, WWE SmackDown! Here Comes the Pain is one of the most beloved entries in the long-running WWE SmackDown! video game series. Developed by Yukes and published by THQ, the game marked a significant leap in gameplay, presentation, and content from its predecessors. A departure from the more arcadey style of earlier titles, Here Comes the Pain introduced a deeper, more realistic wrestling experience that appealed to both casual fans of WWE and die-hard wrestling enthusiasts. In this review, we will explore the game's story mode, gameplay mechanics, roster, presentation, and lasting impact on the wrestling video game genre.

Story Mode: The Road to Glory

The core of WWE SmackDown! Here Comes the Pain is its engaging and action-packed story mode. While the game doesn't feature a scripted, linear storyline like modern wrestling games, it offers a compelling career mode that immerses players into the world of WWE. Players can create their own custom wrestler and guide them through a series of matches to achieve WWE superstardom. The goal is to earn the title of WWE Champion by competing in various matches across both brands, SmackDown! and Raw.

What makes the story mode unique is the level of depth and interaction that the game offers. Players can choose from a variety of match types, such as regular matches, tag team bouts, and more extreme stipulations like Hell in a Cell and TLC. The game is structured in a way that allows the player to earn respect and popularity, with choices impacting the development of the storyline. While the path to the championship is linear, the decisions made along the way affect rivalries, alliances, and title opportunities.

One of the key aspects of the career mode is the way it emphasizes the creation and development of a wrestler. The game's customization options allow players to modify their character's appearance, attributes, and even move sets. The character creation mode is extensive, providing a wide range of options to make a truly unique wrestler. These options give the player a sense of ownership over their career, making their in-game successes feel more rewarding. The story mode also includes random backstage segments, creating a sense of unpredictability that adds to the game's replay value.

Gameplay: Refined Mechanics and Dynamic Action

When it comes to gameplay, WWE SmackDown! Here Comes the Pain truly shines. The game delivers a refined wrestling experience that strikes a perfect balance between accessibility and depth. The controls are responsive and easy to learn, yet they allow for a wide variety of moves and maneuvers. The primary gameplay mechanic revolves around performing a combination of strikes, grapples, and taunts to wear down the opponent before finishing them off with signature finishing moves.

One of the standout features of Here Comes the Pain is its fluid combat system. The grapple system is intuitive, allowing players to chain together a series of throws, slams, and submission holds. Players can use both the square and triangle buttons for various strikes, and the circle button is used to initiate grapples. The overall flow of combat is smooth, making each match feel fast-paced and dynamic. The game also features a stamina system, which prevents players from endlessly spamming moves and encourages a more thoughtful approach to matches.

The match types in Here Comes the Pain are varied and well-implemented. Traditional one-on-one matches are complemented by tag team matches, elimination matches, and more extreme stipulations like ladder matches, cage matches, and the infamous Hell in a Cell. Each match type comes with its own set of rules and strategies, adding variety and replayability. The inclusion of weapons and the ability to use the environment to your advantage—whether by throwing your opponent into the steel steps or smashing them with a steel chair—adds an exciting element to the gameplay. The dynamic nature of the match design means that no two matches are the same, keeping the gameplay fresh and engaging.

Another notable feature is the inclusion of a stamina system. Wrestlers tire as they perform moves, meaning that players must manage their energy carefully throughout a match. This prevents spamming and makes matches feel more realistic, encouraging players to plan their moves and time their attacks strategically. Additionally, the use of submission holds is more prominent, offering players the option to tap out their opponents in a match. This adds another layer of depth and strategy to the gameplay, making Here Comes the Pain a wrestling game that rewards tactical thinking.

The Roster: Legends and Modern Superstars

WWE SmackDown! Here Comes the Pain boasts an impressive roster of over 50 WWE superstars, including legends, current stars, and some hidden characters. The roster features well-known names from both SmackDown! and Raw, such as The Rock, Stone Cold Steve Austin, Triple H, Kurt Angle, The Undertaker, Brock Lesnar, and many others. Each wrestler is faithfully recreated with their signature moves, entrance animations, and finishing maneuvers.

One of the standout features of the roster is the inclusion of classic wrestlers from WWE’s past. Fans of the Attitude Era and the earlier years of WWE will appreciate the chance to play as superstars like The Rock, “Stone Cold” Steve Austin, and Shawn Michaels. These legends are not only part of the game's storylines, but they are also available in various match types, allowing players to relive some of the most iconic moments in WWE history. This inclusion adds a layer of nostalgia for long-time WWE fans, while newer players can still appreciate the wide variety of modern wrestlers on the roster.

In addition to the main roster, players can unlock secret characters, arenas, and even additional movesets. The game’s unlockable content further extends the replay value, offering players incentives to keep playing after completing the main story mode. The sheer variety of wrestlers, along with the deep customization options available in the game, ensures that every player will have a wrestler that suits their playstyle, whether they prefer high-flying moves, powerful slams, or technical wrestling.

Presentation: Authentic WWE Experience

Visually, WWE SmackDown! Here Comes the Pain is one of the best-looking wrestling games of its generation. The character models are highly detailed, and the entrances are faithfully recreated, complete with pyrotechnics and WWE-style theatrics. Wrestlers' signature movements, taunts, and catchphrases are all present, adding to the authenticity of the experience. The arenas are also well-designed, with each one featuring its own unique atmosphere, from the brightly lit SmackDown! stage to the darker, more intense atmosphere of the Hell in a Cell cage.

The attention to detail is evident in the way the wrestlers look and move. Each character’s facial expressions, body movements, and animations are incredibly lifelike, helping to bring the action to life. The crowds are animated and react to the action, creating an atmosphere that feels like a real WWE event. The commentary, provided by Michael Cole and Tazz, adds to the excitement of the matches, with the announcers reacting to the in-ring action in real-time. One of the best aspects of the presentation is the entrance sequences. The game goes all out in recreating the spectacle of WWE entrances, with each wrestler’s signature walk to the ring, complete with their theme music, lighting, and pyrotechnics. These entrances are highly detailed and immersive, adding to the overall spectacle of the game. The music in Here Comes the Pain also contributes to the atmosphere, with a strong soundtrack that includes both WWE’s iconic entrance themes and various hard rock tracks that add to the intensity of the matches.

Lasting Impact and Legacy

WWE SmackDown! Here Comes the Pain is widely regarded as one of the best wrestling video games of all time, and for good reason. Its combination of deep gameplay mechanics, a massive roster of wrestlers, and realistic presentation set a new standard for wrestling games. The game’s success helped to shape the future of the SmackDown! series, with many of the mechanics and features introduced in Here Comes the Pain carrying over into future games.

The impact of Here Comes the Pain on the wrestling game genre cannot be overstated. The game’s dynamic match types, customizable roster, and immersive presentation made it a favorite among both casual players and hardcore wrestling fans. Even years after its release, Here Comes the Pain remains a classic, and it is often cited as the high point of the SmackDown! series. The game’s influence can be seen in subsequent WWE games, and it set the bar for what a wrestling game should strive to achieve.

Conclusion: A Wrestling Game for the Ages

WWE SmackDown! Here Comes the Pain is an exceptional wrestling game that continues to hold up as one of the best in the genre. Its engaging career mode, refined gameplay mechanics, impressive roster, and authentic presentation all contribute to its enduring legacy. Whether you are a long-time fan of WWE or a newcomer to the world of wrestling video games, Here Comes the Pain offers an experience that is both thrilling and immersive. With its perfect balance of accessibility and depth, Here Comes the Pain remains a must-play for any wrestling fan and a timeless classic in the world of video games.
